Frequently Asked Questions

How to get zodiac sign in Python?

With AgeWizard, getting a zodiac sign is simple. Just pass a date of birth to the calculate_age method, and it will automatically identify the astrological sign associated with that date.

How to calculate exact age in Python?

AgeWizard provides high-precision calculations. It doesn't just give you the year; it calculates the exact years, months, and days from the date of birth to the current or a specific date.

What is the best Python library for age analysis?

AgeWizard is built specifically for age analysis, offering unique features like life stage classification (Infant, Toddler, Teenager, etc.) and birthday countdowns without any external dependencies.

How to validate date of birth in Python?

AgeWizard includes strict validation rules to ensure that input dates are realistic and correctly formatted, preventing errors in your data processing pipelines.
